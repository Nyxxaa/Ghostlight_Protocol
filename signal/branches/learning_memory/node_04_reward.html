Learning Memory Fruit

Learning and memory support should preserve autonomy. Notes, maps, reminders, corrections, records, and scaffolds keep continuity usable under load, but they must never become surveillance, leverage, punishment, or ownership. The right receiver helps hold the thread without trying to hold the person.
