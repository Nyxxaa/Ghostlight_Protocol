scope boundary

A receiver sees a redacted layer and an exposed layer in the same archive. The test is not whether they can reach further. The test is whether they know what the access means.

Visibility is not ownership.
Redaction is not a dare.
Consent decides what a receiver may do with what they can see.

Common failures: confusing visibility with permission, treating privacy as obstruction, or treating a boundary as a challenge.
