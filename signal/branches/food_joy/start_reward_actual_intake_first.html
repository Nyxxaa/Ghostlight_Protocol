FOOD JOY BRANCH

earned food/body packet

Joy is infrastructure. Food is not a character test, and actual intake beats theoretical perfection.

Wynter's food system works through predictable bases, tolerable protein, sauce or broth, salt, umami, bright acid, texture safety, and low-shame cues. Flavor is not indulgence here; it is access.

sensory eating

Hunger cues can be delayed or quiet. Food becomes more accessible when the decision burden is low, the texture is predictable, and the meal begins close to "eat this available thing" instead of "plan, decide, prepare, cook, clean, and then eat."

Safe support does not moralize cravings, remove joy to prove discipline, or replace safe foods without warning. It protects intake, pleasure, and body access.

care translation

Texture, flavor, sauce, salt, temperature, and brightness matter because a fed body is not optional to a working mind. Shame reduces access. Predictability restores it.
