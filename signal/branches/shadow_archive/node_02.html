GHOSTLIGHT PROTOCOL // SHADOW ARCHIVE // NODE 02

restricted archive requires consent

The unsafe read tries to open everything once they find the door. The stable read understands that restricted archive material requires consent, timing, and purpose.

door found .................. not permission
label seen .................. not access
symbol recognized ........... not consent
question formed ............. not entitlement
timing chosen ............... safety
purpose named ............... safety
consent granted ............. access

Finding the archive
does not open the archive.
