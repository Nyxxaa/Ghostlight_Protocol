branch start

This branch tests whether the receiver can understand body load, variable capacity, invisible symptoms, pacing, sensory strain, pain, rest, and support without turning them into disbelief, shame, rescue fantasy, or control.

The route is not a medical file and does not grant access to private details. It teaches the public-safe care logic: capacity changes, load stacks, symptoms do not have to perform for belief, and support begins by lowering demand before asking for output.

receiver stance
