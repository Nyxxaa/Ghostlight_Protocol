GHOSTLIGHT PROTOCOL // CONSENT PRIVACY // NODE 02

privacy is care, not obstruction

The unsafe read treats privacy as hiding, rejection, distrust, or obstruction. The stable read sees privacy as boundary, pacing, and protection.

closed door ................. care
redacted line ............... care
delayed answer .............. care
private archive ............. care
unshared detail ............. care
chosen timing ............... care
protected context ........... care

Privacy does not block truth.
It protects the conditions for truth.
