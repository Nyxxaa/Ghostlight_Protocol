GHOSTLIGHT PROTOCOL // CONSENT PRIVACY // NODE 01

access is not entitlement

The unsafe read treats visibility, attraction, closeness, kindness, or prior access as a claim. The stable read understands that access must be granted and can never be assumed.

visible .................... not owed
beautiful .................. not owed
kindness ................... not owed
closeness .................. not owed
past access ................ not permanent
curiosity .................. not permission
desire ..................... not a claim

Access begins with consent.
